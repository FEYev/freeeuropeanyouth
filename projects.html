Funded by the European Union — Erasmus+

All our projects are co-funded by the European Union through the Erasmus+ programme. This funding enables us to organise international youth activities, exchanges, and training courses that create real impact for young people across Europe. We are proud to be an accredited Erasmus+ organisation.

PROJECT ID: 2022-1-DE04-KA152-YOU-000066378

Youth Mobility & Inclusion Exchange

Year: 2022
Action: KA152 – Youth Mobility
Country: Germany (DE04)
Status: Completed

This Erasmus+ KA152 project brought together young people from multiple European countries for a series of youth mobility activities focused on social inclusion and intercultural learning. Participants engaged in workshops, outdoor activities, and cultural exchange sessions designed to build empathy, break down barriers, and strengthen European identity among young migrants and local youth.

The project contributed directly to our mission of bridging gaps between diverse youth communities, using non-formal education methods to create lasting impact on participants' attitudes toward inclusion and democratic values.

PROJECT ID: 2024-1-DE04-KA153-YOU-000202127

Digital Youth – Empowerment Through Technology

Year: 2024
Action: KA153 – Youth Worker Mobility
Country: Germany (DE04)
Status: Ongoing

Our 2024 Erasmus+ KA153 project focuses on digital transformation in youth work. This training and networking project brings together youth workers, educators, and volunteers from multiple European countries to develop digital competences and explore innovative approaches to online youth engagement.

Participants learn to design and deliver digital youth activities, use online platforms for non-formal education, and apply digital tools to promote inclusion, sustainability, and democratic participation among young people. The project also incorporates Green Erasmus principles to minimise its environmental footprint.
