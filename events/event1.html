Youth Exchange – Digital Futures

An Erasmus+ international youth exchange exploring the digital world together.

💻
Date: 2024
Location: Germany
Type: Erasmus+ KA152
Participants: 30 young people
Countries: 5 European countries

The "Digital Futures" youth exchange brought together 30 young people from five European countries for a 7-day intensive programme focused on digital literacy, online safety, and how technology can be used as a tool for positive social change.

About the Event

In an increasingly digital world, we believe every young person deserves the tools and confidence to navigate online environments safely and use technology creatively. This exchange was designed using non-formal education methods — with workshops, team challenges, and hands-on activities replacing traditional lectures.

Participants came from diverse backgrounds — some highly confident with technology, others taking their first steps online — but all left with new skills, new friends, and a broader European perspective.

Activities & Highlights

  • Digital storytelling workshop — participants created short videos about their communities
  • Online safety simulation — exploring misinformation, privacy, and digital rights
  • Social media for activism — how to use platforms for positive change
  • Intercultural evenings — each country shared food, music, and traditions
  • Closing presentation — groups presented their digital projects to local youth workers

Impact & Outcomes

Participants left the exchange with improved digital skills, stronger intercultural awareness, and a lasting network of European peers. Several participants went on to run digital workshops in their home communities, sharing what they had learned.

"This was the first time I felt truly at home in a group of people from different countries. The digital workshops changed how I think about my phone and social media." — Participant, Poland
