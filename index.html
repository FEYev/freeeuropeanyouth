What We Do

Our Focus Areas

We work across seven key pillars to create real change for young people in Europe.

🌍

Social Inclusion

Bridging gaps between migrant and local youth through shared activities.

🌿

Sustainability

Promoting Green Erasmus and eco-conscious youth projects across Europe.

💻

Digital Transformation

Equipping youth with digital skills for the future of work and society.

🗳️

Democratic Participation

Encouraging young people to engage in civic life and democratic processes.

💼

Youth Employability

Building skills and confidence for young people entering the labour market.

❤️

Well-being & Health

Supporting healthy, balanced lifestyles and mental well-being for youth.

🤝

Intercultural Exchange

Celebrating diversity and building solidarity between European youth.

✨

Personal Development

Non-formal education to grow confidence, leadership, and creativity.

About Us

A Grassroots Movement for European Youth

Free European Youth e.V. was born from a grassroots initiative in 2020 and officially established as a legal NGO in 2021. We connect migrant and local youth across Europe, creating spaces for growth, dialogue, and action.

🌏

Inclusive by Design

We create programs where every young person feels welcomed and valued.

📚

Non-Formal Education

Learning through doing — workshops, mobilities, and real-world projects.

🌱

Sustainability First

We embed Green Erasmus principles in all our activities.
